Welcome to Home Console History!

It is no secret that video games play a sizable role in pop-culture today, which has come with the popularity of home video game consoles. Over the past ~50 years or so, dozens of video game consoles have come and gone with much of their history left scattered across various websites on the internet. Through this website I take a look at the history of home video game consoles and the major events that shaped the current video game landscape today. Checkout the table of contents below, and have fun!
