The 1990's

The 1990's would mark a big turning point when it comes to video game technologies. During this decade 3D graphics would go from pretty much unhear of, to the standard all consoles should have.


Some of the early adopters to 3D technology would be companies like Sony, who where just getting into the gaming industry at this point.
