The 1970's

The 70's would end up being an important time for videos games as during this decade, both arcades and home video games would come into popularity.


The first home console release came in 1972 with the Magnavox Odyssey. The Oddysey was far from the type of console you'd see today, as it had incredably simple, black and white graphics.
